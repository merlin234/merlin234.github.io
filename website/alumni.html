Join the AUM Alumni Association

Welcome home! As a member of AUM’s Alumni Association, you’re part of a community that plays a vital role in making AUM an even stronger university. Your membership can help us award hundreds of thousands of dollars in undergraduate scholarships, fund initiatives to retain exceptional faculty, support graduate scholarships and more.

Join online or complete the Alumni Membership Form and mail it to us. You can become a Lifetime Member with a one-time membership fee of $500, or with five annual payments of $100.

Benefits to Joining the AUM Alumni Association

  • Receive discounts and savings on anything from pizza and the zoo, to movie tickets and car rentals as part of Abenity Perks Program
  • Free admission to AUM Athletic events
  • Get a 15 percent discount on services and a 10 percent discount on devices at AUM Speech and Hearing Clinic for members and their immediate family.
  • Receive a 25 percent discount on Warhawk Fit, the only cardiovascular disease prevention program of its kind in the River Region.
  • Get help with your job search, resume assistance, and interview preparation from our Career Development Center
  • As an AUM Alumni Association member, you’ll have access to complimentary tickets for Theatre AUM productions.
  • Receive a 10 percent discount at the AUM Bookstore.
  • Each semester, get a $10 discount on any one personal development course offered by AUM Continuing Education & Community Engagement.
  • Get a 10 percent discount at Delta Printing & Sign Co. in Montgomery.

Alumni Professorship Awards

The Alumni Association sponsors Alumni Professorship Awards to recognize outstanding AUM faculty who best exemplify excellence in teaching, research, scholarly and creative accomplishments and service.

Graduate Study Merit Scholarships

The Graduate Study Merit Scholarships are awarded to qualified AUM students who maintain a GPA of 3.4 or higher, complete nine hours each semester and be continuously enrolled for each semester covered by the award.

Outstanding Faculty Service Award

The Alumni Association recognizes outstanding AUM faculty with this award. To be eligible, faculty must have five or more years of full-time service at AUM. Recipients receive $1,000.
